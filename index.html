About Me

I am a 21 year-old undergraduate student at the University of Florida, pursuing a major in Computer Science and a minor in Leadership. I was born in Havana, Cuba, and came to the United States at the age of 3. Throughout my life, I have always held to the belief that through hard-work and perseverance, anything can be accomplished and one's dreams fulfilled. I am driven by a desire to achieve my fullest potential, make this world a better place any way that I can, and find some measure of happiness in my life.

Some of my personal interests include playing and watching team sports. Sports that I watch include Hockey, Football, Basketball, Soccer, and Water Polo. I am also fond of playing Soccer, Baseball, flag Football, and Basketball. Going on jogs, fishing, and bowling are included in my list of hobbies. However, my favorite pastime is spending time with friends and family, as they are the most valuable part of my life.

My ultimate career goal is to become an enterprise architect / project manager, leading and directing teams within an organization. My aim is to direct teams in accomplishing goals that improve the reputation of the company and give individual team members a sense of satisfaction that their work is instrumental to the success of the business and the betterment of society. I want to help team members feel as though they are a part of something special, make them develop a deeper level of emotional intelligence, and contribute to the overall well-being of both society and the company which I am a part of.
